About ShopCiti

ShopCiti offers local South African stores the opportunity to host their stores virtually on our website by paying a monthly fee for this service. Users can easily find and purchase products from their favorite local South African stores, all in one place.

This gives local brands exposure to customers all across South Africa. Our website was launched to serve South African local brands and customers.

Services

Our services provide a professional and easy-to-use interface for online shopping and entrepreneurs who would like to showcase their amazing work and talent.
